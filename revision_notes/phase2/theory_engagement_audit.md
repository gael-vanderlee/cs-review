## Use in manuscript

These counts support a corpus-grounded statement that sensory-conflict language is the most frequent theoretical framing in the eligible studies, but that most papers use theory to motivate design or background rather than to interpret results. The Results rewrite in Phase 5 should therefore use sensory-conflict, postural-instability, vection/unexpected-vection, and display-specific mechanisms as interpretive lenses rather than treating all reported neuromarkers as interchangeable correlates.
